## So, according to what I found, read.table does some modification in the column names by adding a dot '.' where there are spaces originally... read_delim and other readr options don't do
that, and it seems that MSstats require it the input files to have this kind of names... So... Always use base-r options for reading files when interested on using them as MSstats input.
